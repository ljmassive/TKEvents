Outdoor Events

If you’ve ever struggled to get everyone to hear you at a busy event, or been asked the same question over and over again, this is where our team can help.

We can supply large speakers that support a full dynamic range - no tinniness when used to play music as with horn type speakers. We have a caravan that has been converted into a commentary/DJ booth/race office, sound mixers, microphones and radio microphones for commentary or announcements.

Below you'll find just a few examples of the work we've done. Please don’t hesitate to get in touch for pricing and to talk about any part of your event!

We supported this kayak race on the Conwy with the use of the caravan as a site office for around £50.

We supplied this horse event in Ruthin our caravan for use as the event office for less than £100 for the day..

We supported this windsurfing event with outdoor sound, commentary, DJing, and some tents at the lakeside for around £300 for the day.

We supplied speakers, lights, our caravan, setup, and three evenings of sound and DJ support to this outdoor cinema in Colwyn Bay for £650.

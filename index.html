WHAT WE DO

We are a sound and lighting company based between Colwyn Bay and Cardiff. We can help you with speakers and lights for weddings, outdoor events, parties, and bespoke events.

Parties

We have a huge range of party equipment available for hire. We have supplied the lights, speakers, stands, mixers, and microphones to anything from decent sized house parties, right through to hangar sized spaces, from city centres, to fields in the middle of nowhere.

Outdoor Events

Having trouble finding reliable support for outdoor events? Want to make sure your event looks professional and keep spectators and competitors informed?

Our friendly team has all the experience and equipment required to make sure your event runs smoothly, and looks and sounds great.

Weddings

Getting married in a location that is perfect for you but worried about your setup? TK events can help with the lighting and sound to get your special day to go perfectly.
